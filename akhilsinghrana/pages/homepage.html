Introduction

I am a Senior Machine Learning Engineer with extensive experience in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Generative AI, Remote Sensing, and Earth Observation. My expertise spans across foundational models, vision transformers, self-supervised learning, and deep reinforcement learning research.

As a Senior Machine Learning Scientist at Planet Labs Germany GmbH, I released RapidAI4EO , a corpus of dense time series satellite imagery. Developed a Change Detection product to update Corine Land Cover and Land Use classes using self-supervised learning models like BYOL and DINO. I also engineered pipelines for training complex deep neural networks using contrastive learning based methods, applied self-supervised learning techniques in the agriculture domain for crop classification and phenology phase detection, and created multi-modal models for spatio-temporal Earth Observation data.

Prior to that, I worked as a Senior Machine Learning Researcher at Avira, where I directed end-to-end development of face and identity anonymization products using generative AI and face synthesis, and worked on Anomaly detection in network traffic DDOS using Auto Encoders.

At Airbus Defence and Space, I led GAN approaches on TerraSAR-X satellite data, improving model performance by 25% by changing network Architecture and adding custom loss functions. I also conducted deep reinforcement learning study for Airbus Defense, achieving a 30% improvement in mission success rates for smart UAVs, served as AI/DataLab Ambassador, promoting AI initiatives and fostering collaboration, and supervised students with their master thesis.

As a Top Rated Freelance Developer at Upwork, I assisted over 5 startups as a consultant and full-stack developer, delivering 100% of projects on time, contributing to a 98% increase in client satisfaction.
